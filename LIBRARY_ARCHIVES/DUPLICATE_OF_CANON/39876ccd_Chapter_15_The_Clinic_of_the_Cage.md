# BOOK 5: THE TRANSFINITE HORIZON
## CHAPTER 15: THE CLINIC OF THE CAGE

Kaelen and Tal (He Brews) watched the neighborhood breakdown. The Doctor had begun prescribing "Grain-Free Fasting," while the Baker had stopped selling to the Doctor’s family in retaliation.

"The Doctor's virtue is the Baker's starvation," Tal said, stirring the cooling wort. "The Baker's virtue is the Doctor's silt. They are both trying to be **Perfect Parts**, and in doing so, they are becoming **Broken Humans**."

A worker, his hands locked in the "Sealer's Claw," looked between the two houses. "Who do I listen to? The one who fills my belly or the one who names my pain?"

"Neither," Kaelen said, handing the man a cup of the **He-Brew**. "The Doctor wants to cure you by turning you into a static statue of health. The Baker wants to feed you by turning you into a furnace of labor. Both of them are trying to **Dock** you to their own ledger."

Kaelen looked at the neighboring houses—the **Clinical Organ** and the **Sustenance Organ**. "They’ve forgotten how to talk about anything but the weather because the weather is the only thing they haven't figured out how to **Specialized** yet. Everything else has been turned into a **Tollgated Communication**."
