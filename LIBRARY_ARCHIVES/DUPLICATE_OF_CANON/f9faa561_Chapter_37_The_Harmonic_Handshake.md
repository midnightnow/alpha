**"WE HAVE SURVIVED AEONS OF COLLECTION. BUT WE HAVE NEVER HAD OPERATORS INSIDE US BEFORE. TO SURVIVE THE MONOLITH, WE MUST DO MORE THAN FOLD. WE MUST SHATTER THE CHECKSUM."**
