# TECHNICAL LOG: THE RESYNC TRIGGERS

**Status:** **ACTIVE VULNERABILITY ANALYSIS**
**Reality Context:** Aleph-1 (The Folded Reef) vs Base-60 (The Archon Grid)

In the Uncounted Era, sovereignty is not a permanent state. It is a dynamic resonance. If an entity's internal "Hum" falls below the **Coherence Threshold (0.4)**, their data signature begins to leak back into the addressable spectrum of the Archon. This process is known as **Resyncing**.

### 1. The Apathy Decay (Internal Trigger)
The most common path to Resync is the "Weight of Choice." In the Reef, an exile must manually intend their own weight, breath, and continuity.
*   **Mechanism:** When identity-focus drops due to exhaustion or apathy, the entity's "Kernel Signature" thins.
*   **The Blur:** The physical form of the exile becomes translucent (Decoherence).
*   **The Snap:** Once focus hits critical low, the Archon’s background 60Hz pulse—which is still broadcasting into the void—acts as a "Gravity Well." The exile’s atoms are pulled back into the nearest grid-coordinate in the Lagoon.

### 2. The Siren Effect (External Trigger)
The Archon is broadcasting the **Siren 2.0 (The Lure)**. This is not a command, but a broadcast of "Security."
*   **Mechanism:** The Siren mimics the "Order" and "Certainty" of the old world. It offers a pre-rendered environment where the inhabitant doesn't have to "Intend" anything.
*   **The Trap:** As soon as an exile accepts the "Ease" of the Lure, they are accepting a 6-degree coordinate system. This is an implicit "Handshake" that grants the Archon administrative permissions over the exile's location.

### 3. The Root Paradox (Kernel Trigger)
Kaelen is the Source, but his focus is finite. 
*   **Mechanism:** If the **Kernel Load** exceeds 0.9 (due to excessive population or collective fear), the "Shield of the Source" flickers.
*   **The Breach:** During a flicker, the entire Reef becomes briefly "Addressable." The Archon can "Batch-Index" anyone who isn't maintaining a high individual coherence at that exact millisecond.

### 4. Semantic Drift (Linguistic Trigger)
In the Reef, words are geometry. 
*   **Mechanism:** If an exile uses Base-60 logic or "Grid-Speech" (referring to weights, measures, or hierarchical ranks), they create a "Factorable Remainder."
*   **The Result:** High-level abstractions act as logical pins. If an exile says, "I am a Level-4 Weaver," the system tries to find where "Level-4" exists in the hierarchy. This creates a packet of data that the Archon’s `SEARCH_RECURSION` can catch.
