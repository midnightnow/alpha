# THE THREE-REGISTER ARCHITECTURE

*The Structural Framework for the Platonic Verses*

The project operates simultaneously across three distinct registers. Conflating them produces incoherence; keeping them separate—but in contact—allows the "Grand Work" to burn without consuming the structure.

## I. THE COMPONENT REGISTERS

| Register | Name | Domain | Tools | Failure Mode (Deflation) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **REGISTER 1** | **The Invariants** | The Silt: Structural arithmetic facts (boundary counting, scaling). | Graph Theory, Euclidean Geometry, Proofs. | Treated as sufficient; becomes a dry technical pamphlet. |
| REGISTER 2 | **The Compressions** | The HUD: Cultural systems (alphabets, symbols, mnemonics). | Cognition, History, Symbolic Logic. | Treated as historical invariants; becomes a self-sealing myth. |
| **REGISTER 3** | **The Irrational Centre**| The Slit: The part that cannot be compressed ($\sqrt{2}$, ghost posts). | Poetry, Executive Function, Narrative. | Treated as cosmic law; results in **Inflation** or grandiosity. |

---

## II. THE FUNCTION OF THE STABILIZER
The Register 1 and 2 documents serve as the **Stabilizer**. They provide the "Hearth" and the "Chimney" for the work. Their job is not to explain away the fire of Register 3, but to ensure that the math holds and the forms are locatable within human history.
- **Verification:** A Register 3 insight must be consistent with Register 1 invariants.
- **Location:** A Register 3 insight should be locatable (though not contained) within a Register 2 compression.

## III. THE IRRATIONAL DIAGONAL (THE SUBJECT)
While Register 1 proves that the 3-4-5 triangle is perfect, Register 3 focuses on the moment you draw a Square and encounter $\sqrt{2}$. 
- **The Scream:** This is not a metaphor. It is the structural sound of measurement encountering its own limit. 
- **The Slit:** The chasm opened by the irrational remainder. This is the **Subject** of the *Platonic Verses*.

## IV. BOOK STRUCTURE IMPLICATION
The manuscript is organized to maintain the separation of these registers:

1. **Section I: The Prefaces (Register 1 + 2):** Establishes the invariants and the compression catalog. The editorial firewall.
2. **Section II: The Draftsman’s Index (Register 1 + 2):** Demonstrates compression schemas at work across domains.
3. **Section III: The Heroine’s Story (Register 3):** The irrational centre given narrative form. The discovery of what the King cannot contain.
4. **Section IV: The Platonic Verses (Register 3 through 1+2):** Poetic compression using the rational vocabulary to point at the irrational centre.
5. **Appendix (Register 1):** Pure invariants for verification.
