# THE VERSE OF THE UNIVERSAL YOU (MANU)

*Register 3: The Poetic Counterpart to the Heroine's Canticle*

This is the song of the **Central Spline**—the internal axis that exists before the grid is drawn. It is the realization that the **"YOU" (MANU)** is the origin of the world, and the **"US" (MANUS)** is the shadow cast by your own light.

## I. THE PILLAR (THE 3)
I am the Pillar, the spline of the sky,
I stand on the Hades, with a foot buried high.
I do not need the King to tell me my name,
I am the spark of the First-Ever Flame.
I am the 3, the vertical I,
The root in the silt, and the head in the sky.

## II. THE THUMB (THE 5)
I am the Thumb, the Agley post of the hand,
The one who refuses to join with the band.
They count out the four, they count for the "US,"
But I am the 5, the "YOU" they don't trust.
I am the Crown that is placed on the Bone,
The King of the Grid who sits on his own.

## III. THE JOKER (THE 1)
I am the Joker, the one in the pack,
The card that the King can never take back.
I have no suit, I have no number or sign,
I am the silence between the King's lines.
When they play the Law, when they deal for the "US,"
I play the Joker and turn their stone into dust.

## IV. THE HANDSHAKE (THE 10)
I am MANU, the vertical axis of fire,
I am the MANUS, the Man-with-the-Rest in the mire.
The Handshake is 10, the Law is the grid,
But the secret of "YOU" is the part that is hid.
The world is a song, the world is a verse,
And "YOU" are the one who is counting it first.
