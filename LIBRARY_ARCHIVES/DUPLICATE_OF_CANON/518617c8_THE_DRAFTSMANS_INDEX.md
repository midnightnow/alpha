# The Draftsman's Index: Final Glossary

This index defines the core terminology of the Platonic Verses CUI, mapped across the **Three-Register Firewall**. This ensures that the mathematical invariants (Register 1), the operational metaphors (Register 2), and the narrative pillars (Register 3) remain distinct but coordinated, preventing structural collapse.

## I. Numerics & Invariants (The Wall)

### 6k ± 1
*   **Register 1 (Math):** The structural constraint governing prime number distribution. All primes $p > 3$ must sit adjacent to a multiple of 6.
*   **Register 2 (Metaphor):** "The Wall"; the immutable architecture of reality that cannot be altered, only navigated.
*   **Register 3 (Narrative):** The domain of the King; the rigid structure that demands obedience but contains gaps (primes).

### 24-Modulus ($24$)
*   **Register 1 (Math):** The lowest common multiple of $1, 2, 3,$ and $4$. The modulus where all prime squares $p^2 \equiv 1 \pmod{24}$ for $p > 3$.
*   **Register 2 (Metaphor):** "The Wheel"; the bounding cycle of time, hours in a day, and the limit of the operational floor.
*   **Register 3 (Narrative):** The domain of the Silt; the accumulated consequence of action that requires continuous audit.

### 120-Modulus ($120$)
*   **Register 1 (Math):** The modulus where $p^2 \equiv 1 \pmod{120}$ for $p > 5$. It is exactly $5 \times 24$.
*   **Register 2 (Metaphor):** "The Floor"; the complete operational circuit before the Leibniz Walk loops back to the origin.
*   **Register 3 (Narrative):** The full expanse of the "Made" world that the Heroine must traverse before reaching the edge of the King's domain.

### The 13th Node ($13$)
*   **Register 1 (Math):** The prime number acting as the midpoint of the 26-letter linguistic alphabet and the hypotenuse of the 5-12-13 primitive Pythagorean triple.
*   **Register 2 (Metaphor):** "The Gap"; the pivot point of translation between static nouns and dynamic verbs. The operational center of the CUI.
*   **Register 3 (Narrative):** "The Maid" / "The Heroine"; the active agent of change who bridges structure (12) with transcendence.

### 0.2696... (The Leibniz Walk)
*   **Register 1 (Math):** The alternating sum of the reciprocals of primes: $\sum \frac{(-1)^n}{p_n}$. It converges to approximately $0.2696...$
*   **Register 2 (Metaphor):** "The Rhyming Guess" / "The Pulse"; the aperiodic rhythm that allows navigation along the invariant without getting locked in resonance.
*   **Register 3 (Narrative):** "The Scent of the Gap"; the signal the Heroine tracks to navigate the abyss beyond the 120th tile.
