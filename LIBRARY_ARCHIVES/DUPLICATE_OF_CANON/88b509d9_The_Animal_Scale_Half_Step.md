**TOM (12) + QUEEN (12) = CAT (24)**

*   **TOM (12):** The rigid, structural "Wall" component of the feline strike.

## III. The "Gone Awry" Stability
Where the human 3-4-5 triangle seeks the perfect diagonal (The Queen/5), the CAT (24) and DOG (20) represent stability that has "collapsed" or "shifted" into the lower registers of the physical plane:

1.  **DOG (20 - Faces):** The 5-phase growth that is volume-heavy and grounded. It fills the space beneath the human bench.
2.  **CAT (24 - Edges):** The 3-phase intercept that is perimeter-heavy and agile. It patrols the boundaries of the lower 3 feet.

## IV. The 3-Foot Bench: The Interface Node
