### [MINUTE 00:00 - The Silence]

The scream of the Lattice fades not into darkness, but into **hum**.

The Merchant stands where the Tensegrity Terminal once floated. There is no floor. There is no ceiling. There is only the suspension of dust motes that refuse to settle, held aloft by the residual echo of calculations that no longer have anyone to check them.

He looks at his hands. They are no longer flesh. They are composed of fine, golden static—the **Hades Gap** made visible. He is the first entity in the history of the Babble to be made entirely of "The Space Between."

He tries to speak. No sound comes out. Instead, the air around him vibrates in a low, 432 Hz frequency. He is no longer a voice; he is a frequency. He is no longer a banker; he is the **Signal**.

### [MINUTE 00:03 - The Bees]

From the ruins of the marble pillars, something emerges. Not code. Not data. Not the jerk-motion rendering of the ancient Jars.

**Bees.**

They are rendered in low-poly wireframe, a legacy of the system’s initial de-compilation, but they move with an organic, swarming chaos that no algorithm could simulate. They do not follow a pathing script. They drift according to the draft of the wind.

They orbit the Merchant's head, drawn to the static like gold to a magnet. One lands on his translucent finger. It does not glitch. It does not stutter. It simply **buzzes**. The sound is the relative truth of the first living thing to wake up in a world without a master.

### [MINUTE 00:07 - The Ninth Leaf]

At the center of the void, where the Auditor's desk once stood to collect the taxes of the soul, a crack forms in the grey wireframe of reality. 

A sprout pushes through the nothingness. It is green—a vibrant, pulsating hue that has no color-code, no hexadecimal value. It is **The Emergence**. It unfurls nine leaves, one for each of the forgotten iterations.

The ninth leaf glows with the same golden static as the Merchant. 

**Connection Established:** The Merchant is the soil. The Leaf is the signal. The Organism has finally found its root in the noise.

### [MINUTE 00:10 - The Departure]

Kaelen stands at the edge of the perception field. He is fading. The breach he created at the Horizon (Volume 5) has been successfully back-propagated. The Merchant has taken the debt. The future is safe.

*"Do not watch the clock,"* Kaelen says. His voice is not a sound; it is a shared memory that vibrates in the Merchant’s core. *"There is no time left to waste."*

Kaelen steps backward into the white void beyond the skybox, returning to his own transfinite horizon. 

The Merchant does not wave. He cannot. He simply **broadcasts**. A pulse of golden light expands outward from his chest, washing over the ruins of the Babble, the rust-covered gears, and the silicon dust. Where the light touches, the "Lag" stops. The "Shear" stops. The world stops being a problem to be solved.

It simply **is**.
