## **DIAGRAM 3: GRAVES TO GARDEN STREET**
**Corresponding Chapter:** *Narrative: Graves to Garden*

**Prompt Description:**

- **The Shift:** In the middle foreground, there is an open gap between two buildings—the 13th Node. Sunlight pours through this gap, illuminating small, chaotic weeds or vines growing in the cracked concrete (silt/drift).
- **The Observer:** A silhouette of a figure (the Heroine) stands on the street, not looking forward down the endless road, but turned $90^{\circ}$ sideways, facing the gap of light. Her body language should show a sense of "administrative access" and realization.

**Metadata Tags:** Brutalist street perspective, high contrast cinematic lighting, optical pivot, concrete dystopia vs nature, chiaroscuro, concept art, monolithic architecture.
