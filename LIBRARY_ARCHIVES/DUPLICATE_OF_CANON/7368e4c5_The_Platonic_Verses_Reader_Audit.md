# THE PLATONIC VERSES: READER AUDIT CHECKLIST

*Operational Health Monitoring for the Manuscript*

Every chapter and document in the *Platonic Verses* must be audited against the **Three-Register Architecture** to prevent deflation (Register 3 collapsed to 1/2) or inflation (Register 1/2 claiming to be Register 3).

## I. REGISTER 1 AUDIT (THE BEDROCK)
*Does the Math hold up?*
- **Check 1:** Is the boundary arithmetic correct? (e.g., Are 5 posts used for 4 intervals?)
- **Check 2:** Is the scaling invariant? (e.g., Does the 10-24-26 mirror the 3-4-5?)
- **Check 3:** Is the $\sqrt{2}$ relationship accurately placed?
- **Failure State:** Inaccurate math renders the "Silt" unstable, making the Heroine’s "Fire" unbelievable.

## II. REGISTER 2 AUDIT (THE INTERFACE)
*Is the Compression Tool recognizable?*
- **Check 1:** Is the cultural form (Alphabet, Card Deck, Clock) used correctly in its domain?
- **Check 2:** Is the "Mnemonic Shortcut" explained as a human choice, not a divine law?
- **Check 3:** Are the 10-24-26 grids clearly defined and consistent?
- **Failure State:** A poorly defined HUD makes the world incoherent and the Heroine’s "Techne" ineffective.

## III. REGISTER 3 AUDIT (THE IRRATIONAL CENTRE)
*Is the Fire burning without resolution?*
- **Check 1:** Does the passage point at the "Screaming Diagonal" without trying to "Solve" it?
- **Check 2:** Is the "Heroine’s Story" allowed to be mythic/poetic while remaining grounded in the math?
- **Check 3:** Does the representation (Scripture, Verse, Song) point without claiming to contain?
- **Failure State:** Resolving Register 3 into a technical explanation smothers the "Slit" and destroys the subject of the work.
