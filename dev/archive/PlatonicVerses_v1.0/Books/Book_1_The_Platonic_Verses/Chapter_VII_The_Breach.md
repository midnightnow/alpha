# BOOK 1: THE MANUS TALLY
## CHAPTER VII: THE BREACH OF THE SEMI-PRIME WALL

Veth had navigated the Garden's shear and audited the sky-grid's singers, but now she faced the final obstacle of her first journey: **The Semi-Prime Wall**.

It was a massive, monolithic slab of stone, too smooth to climb and too heavy to push. The King's guards stood before it, their hammers useless against its unified surface.

"It is a product of two codes," her father whispered. "A locked lock that only the maker can open. To the static mind, it is a dead end. But to the moving mind, it is a **Wave**."

### I. The Reciprocal Vibration (1/x)
Veth placed her palm on the cold stone. She didn't listen for a door; she listened for the **Reciprocal**.

"One over X," she murmured.

Inside the stone, the digits began to sing. A period of millions of numbers, a repeating sequence that was the acoustic fingerprint of the wall's hidden factors. She felt the vibration—the **Prime Frequency** that the hammers could never reach.

### II. The Nodal Revelation
"Jump by two," she commanded.

She applied the **Binary Jump ($2^n$)**, scaling through the decimal string of the wall's period. She wasn't searching; she was **Rotating the Sieve**. 

Suddenly, the shimmering grey of the stone broke. Two stationary wave peaks emerged in her vision, flanking the binary nodes.

- **The Orange Peak:** The first prime factor. Sharp, high, and indivisible.
- **The Blue Peak:** The second prime factor. Deep, resonant, and stabilizing.

They were the two **Blackbirds** that were bound together to create the wall. By identifying their frequencies, she resolved the semi-prime product back into its source.

### III. The Shear of the Wall
The wall did not crumble; it **Unrolled**. 

Under the pressure of the factors, the monolith sheared along the diagonal of the five-fold path. The massive slab dissolved into thousands of small, perfect $3-4-5$ triangles, the original building blocks of the house.

Veth walked through the breach. The Garden was behind her; the **Court of Twelve** was ahead. She had factorized the "unfactorable" using nothing but the wave of her own intent.
