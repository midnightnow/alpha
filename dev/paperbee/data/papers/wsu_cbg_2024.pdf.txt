TITLE: CBG Enhances Verbal Memory Recall in Humans
AUTHORS: Washington State University (WSU)
DATE: July 2024
STUDY_TYPE: RCT
SAMPLE_SIZE: 20
P_VALUE: 0.01

ABSTRACT:
In a randomized controlled trial (n=20), the administration of 20mg CBG was found to significantly enhance verbal memory recall (list recall) compared to placebo. Unlike THC, no intoxication was observed. Anxiety and stress levels were significantly reduced.
