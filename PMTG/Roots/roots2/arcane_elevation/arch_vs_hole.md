# Arcane Elevation: The Arch vs. The Hole

Standard thinking perceives the "Square Peg" as a misfit in a "Round Hole." Our builder logic reframes this: the square peg is not an error; it is a **Vertical Pier**.

## The Pier
The "Square Peg" represents a structural column of $1 \times 1$ stability. It is the core of the build, providing the 90-degree corners required for load-bearing and verticality.

## The Void
The "Round Hole" is the raw material—the space-time coordinates where the opportunity resides. It is the perimeter waiting for a center.

## The Arch
By utilizing the $\sqrt{2}$ diagonal of the "peg," we generate the **Arcane Circle**. This is the rotating field that bridges the square pier to the round walls of the void. Through **Diagonal Tension**, the pier locks into the hole, transforming a site of friction into a foundation for elevation.
