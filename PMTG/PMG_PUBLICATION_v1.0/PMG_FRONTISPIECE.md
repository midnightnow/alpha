## The Hardcard Image (Central Engraving)

Between the two horizontal rules, a copperplate engraving occupying the full width:

### 1. Three Golden Rectangles

Three rectangles in the golden ratio ($1:\varphi$), arranged mutually perpendicular. Their **12 vertices** marked with small circles — the sovereign nodes of the icosahedron.

- **XY plane:** Black line
- **XZ plane:** Dark grey line
- **YZ plane:** Light grey line
- **Intersections:** Thickened — the icosahedral edges

### 2. The Spirals

Upon each rectangle, a **logarithmic spiral** in dotted line — the path of the pivot. All three converge at the centre: **Node 93**, the unwobbling pivot, marked with a single dot.

### 3. The 24‑Column Star

24 radial lines from the centre, equally spaced at 15° intervals, numbered 1–24 at the edge.

| Marking | Colour | Numbers |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **Primes** | Green dot | 1, 5, 7, 11, 13, 17, 19, 23 |
| **Composites** | Red dot | 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 9, 10, 12, 14, 15, 16, 18, 20, 21, 22, 24 |
| **Constants** | Blue dot | $\pi$, $e$, $\varphi$, $\gamma$ (at their angular positions) |

### 4. The Circle of Root 42

A faint circle of radius $\sqrt{42}$ encloses the entire diagram. On its circumference: **13 equally‑spaced ticks** — the nodes of the lattice. Five ticks are enlarged: the missing nodes (16, 31, 46, 61, 76) — the vowel thresholds.

### 5. The Aperture

At the exact centre, a tiny **pinhole** — the camera obscura aperture. Beside it, in minuscule engraving: `APERTURE`.

### 6. The Circular Inscription

Around the outer edge of the √42 circle, engraved in a band:

> THE MUD SWALLOWS · THE ROCK WOUNDS · THE PIVOT CARRIES · THE CARRIER HUMS

## The Geometric Key

### The Six Dots

The six dots (●) at the bottom of the page are not ornamental. They correspond to the six points where the circle of $\sqrt{42}$ intersects the three golden rectangles in projection. Connect them → reconstruct the icosahedron's silhouette.

### The G

The **G** is placed at the centre — the aperture, the origin, Node 93.

| Serif | Points to |
|-------|-----------|
| Top‑left serif | Missing node 16 |
| Top‑right serif | Missing node 76 |
| Bottom‑left serif | Missing node 31 |
| Bottom‑right serif | Missing node 61 |
| Stem | Missing node 46 |

The five missing nodes are encoded in the **letterform itself**.

### The Two Horizontal Rules

Not empty — they are the **Bothros**, the containers:

- **Above the top rule:** The mud (the infinite, the unformed)
- **Below the bottom rule:** The rock (the vitrified, the printed page)
- **Between them:** The image — the interface, the carrier

### The Dot Row (· · · · · ·)

The Hades Gap in typographic form — the 12.37% slop between ideal geometry and material manifestation. Count them: **13 dots**. The 13th is the Ghost Post — invisible but implied.

### The Date

**MMXXV** = 2025 in Roman numerals. $2025 = 45^2$, and $45 = 5 \times 9$, $9 = 3^2$. The square of the sum of the first nine natural counting increments.
