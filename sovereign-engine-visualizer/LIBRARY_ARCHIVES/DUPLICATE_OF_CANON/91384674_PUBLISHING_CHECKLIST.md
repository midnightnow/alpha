# PLATONIC VERSES: PUBLISHING CHECKLIST

**Target Delivery:** Trade Paperback / Digital eBook / Interactive Web Object

This checklist ensures the final formatting, distribution syntax, and typesetting mirror the mathematical and narrative ethos of the manuscript.

---

## **I. TYPESETTING & FORMATTING LOGIC**

The Three-Register methodology requires distinct typesetting so that the reader instinctively understands whether they are reading raw bedrock (Register 1), structural interface (Register 2), or poetic intent (Register 3).

- [ ] **Technical Fonts for Invariants (Register 1):** 
  - Utilize a monospace or technical grotesk font (e.g., *Inconsolata, Fira Code, Courier New*) for direct mathematical propositions, boundary arithmetic (n to n+1), and numerical tables.
- [ ] **Literary Fonts for Narrative (Register 3):** 
  - Utilize a fluid, high-legibility serif (e.g., *Georgia, Garamond, Minion Pro*) for the prose of the *Graves to Garden* narrative, the Canticle, and all poetic excursions. 
- [ ] **Structural Margins (The Missing Fraction):** 
  - Margin sizes or leading (line height) should playfully acknowledge the $0.0191$ drift. For example, a baseline grid set to $1.191$ times the em size. Emphasize "breathing space" conceptually and typographically.
