# The Formula of the Coil

**Axiom 1:** The Cosmos is a Circle (Ω).
**Axiom 2:** The Experience of the Cosmos is a Walk (w).

If one walks in a straight line on a curved surface, one describes a geodesic. If one attempts to walk a straight line on a surface that is expanding due to tension (Hades' Void), one describes a **Spiral**.

The Formula of the Coil (C) is therefore:

$$ C(t) = \Omega \cdot e^{i \cdot (\theta + \frac{1}{2}\pi)} $$

Where:
*   **Ω (Omega)** is the Plate (the constant, the original circle).
*   **t** is the tension (accumulated zeros/debt).
*   **θ (theta)** is the angle of the walk (the "Cord").
*   **i** is the imaginary unit (G.O.A.T./the variable that allows rotation).
