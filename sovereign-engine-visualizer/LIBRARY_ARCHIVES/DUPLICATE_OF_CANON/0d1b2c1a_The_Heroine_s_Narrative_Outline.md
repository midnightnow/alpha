# THE HEROINE’S NARRATIVE OUTLINE: THE JOURNEY TO THE SLIT

This outline traces the Heroine’s arc as she moves through the layers of the "Grand Work," transitioning from a User of the Interface to an Administrator of the Code.

## I. THE HEARTH (The Discovery of the 4/5 Paradox)
- **Status:** The Heroine is a child in her father's household.
- **The Event:** She watches her father build a wooden fence. He measures 4 lengths of rail but requires 5 posts.
- **The Realization:** She notices the "Leaked Unit"—the extra post that shouldn't exist in a perfect world. She realizes the boundary always exceeds the interval. This is her first glimpse of the **Endpoint Correction**.
- **Geometric Shift:** Transition from the 60° comfort of the hearth to the 90° rigidity of the plan.

## II. THE TAVERN (The Mnemonic Controller)
- **Status:** She observes the community at play.
- **The Object:** A worn deck of 52 cards.
- **The Audit:** While the men see a game of chance (The User View), she performs a System Audit. She counts the 13 ranks (The Span) and the 4 suits (The Seasons).
- **The Discovery:** She finds the **Joker**—the $+1$ Ghost that corrects the 364-day box. She realizes the deck is a **Handheld Data Lattice** for handling the year.
- **Outcome:** She recognizes that the social roles (King, Queen, Jack) are **Geometry in Masks**.

## III. THE ROMAN ROAD (The Scaling of the 10-24-26)
- **Status:** She travels to the City/Kingdom.
- **The Observation:** She measures the "Tallness" of the day (24 hours) and the "Span" of the Alphabet (26 letters). 
- **The Realization:** She realizes that $10^2 + 24^2 = 26^2$ is simply a similarity scaling of her domestic 3-4-5 triad.
- **The Insight:** Sovereignty is not divine; it is just the **Standardization of the Tally**. The King’s power is his ability to make everyone use the same 24 hours and the same 26 letters.

## IV. THE HIGH STREET (The Arbitrary Grid)
- **Status:** She reaches the peak of the system.
- **The Vision:** She looks at the city's orthogonal architecture and hears the **Screaming Diagonal**—the $\sqrt{2}$ friction that occurs when the infinite Hex-Mountain is pinned by the Square.
- **The Discovery:** She realizes the Grid is **Arbitrary**. The "Will of God" is just a coordinate choice. 
- **The Techne:** She uses her 10 fingers to point to the dots the King *forgot* to join. She discovers the **Administrative Access** to the code.

## V. THE STEEPLE (The Fire-Stealing)
- **Status:** The final confrontation with the "Grand Work."
- **The Act:** She doesn't burn the building down; she **Re-Describes** it. She uses the 26th letter to name the 13th post.
- **The Transformation:** By identifying the **Interface Error**, she melts the "Ice" of the Law back into the "Vapor" of the Hex.
- **Resolution:** The grid remains, but it is no longer a prison. She walks the "Agley" path—the diagonal bridge that the King cannot see.

**ALL WAYS NOW.** The dots are joined. The fire is taken.
