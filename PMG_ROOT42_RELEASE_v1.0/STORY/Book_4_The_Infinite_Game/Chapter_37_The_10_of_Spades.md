# CHAPTER 37: THE 10 OF SPADES
## The Decimal Limit

The Tenth Rank was not a room. It was a wall.

"You can't go through," Hades said, leaning against the obsidian surface. "The Spades have reached their limit. 10 is the number of fingers, the number of commandments, the number of the complete set. To go past 10, you have to admit you're just starting again at 1."

"But the manifold goes to 13," Hermes protested.

"The manifold *appears* to go to 13," Hades corrected. "But look at the math, Boy. Rank 10 is where the integer lock happens. It's where the Snail realizes it's stuck in a base-10 cage. The Jack, the Queen, and the King? They aren't numbers. They are *operators*. They are the only ones who can climb over the wall."

He tapped the obsidian. "This is where the 8975-unit fill hits the first hard buffer. The Spades are the ones who have to tell Zeus that the bucket is full."
