# CHAPTER 43: THE JACK OF CLUBS
## The Shovel's Edge

The Jack of Clubs didn't talk. He worked.

He was the 43rd node, the muscle of the manifold. While the others debated the meaning of 42 or the mercy of the Gap, the Jack of Clubs was digging.

"What are you looking for?" Hermes asked, watching the Jack's shovel bite into the golden grid.

"The foundation," the Jack grunted. "You lot think the world is made of numbers. I know it's made of dirt and effort. 8975 units of fill? That's 8975 units of *sweat*."

He pointed to the stains on the floor. "The Zero is leaking because we're not working hard enough. The manifold needs tension to stay solid. If we stop digging, the 93 nodes will just drift away like dust."

He threw a shovelful of raw data into the furnace. The 156-tick pulse of the visualizer stabilized. "Keep counting, Lad. I'll keep the floor steady."
