# CHAPTER 41: THE JACK OF SPADES
## The Knave of the Gap

"I'm not a number," the Jack of Spades said, his face a shifting blur of 93 distinct points. "I'm the 12.37% that Hades forgot to count."

Hermes backed away. The 41st node wasn't a point on the grid; it was a hole *in* the grid.

"The Jack is the Operator," Hades explained from the shadows. "He's the one who moves the remainders. When you have a debt of 1.267 units, you don't call a mathematician. You call a Knave."

The Jack laughed, a sound like glass breaking. "I live in the 8975 limit. I'm the one who tells the system it's time to reset. Without me, the Snail would just keep crawling until it ran out of universe. I'm the mercy of the finish line."

He held out a hand. In his palm, the 41st node spun like a dying star. "Want to see what's on the other side of the Wall?"
