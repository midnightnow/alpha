## 🧬 Overview

Mathman in Flatland is a React-based simulation engine designed for **Integrative Medicine** and **Biofield Research**. It bridges the gap between classical physics (linear superposition) and the geometric models of the Human Energy Field (HEF), providing researchers with tools to visualize, quantify, and cite the geometric underpinnings of anatomical fields.

For the comprehensive strategic vision, roadmap, and brief, please consult the **[Project Wiki](./GEMINI_CODE_WIKI.md)**.

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **Physics Engine v2**: Mathematically rigorous wave superposition based on Hecht's *Optics*.
*   **Dimensional Visualization**: Renders Physical, Auric, Haric, and Core Star geometries.
*   **Research Export Pipeline**: Generates JSON-LD (MedicalEntity) and CSV datasets for peer review.
*   **Anatomical Calibration**: Vertical harmonic selector mapped to Chakra stations (Root to Crown).
