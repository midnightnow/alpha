### 1. The Monad (The One)
*   **Function:** `STOP`
*   **Symbol:** The Point (dimensionless).
*   **Nature:** Static, perfect, unchanging limit. It is the compiler that enforces Identity. It defines what a thing *is* by excluding what it is *not*.

### 2. The Indefinite Dyad (The Apeiron)
*   **Function:** `GO`
*   **Symbol:** The Line (infinite direction).
*   **Nature:** The "Great and Small." It is the source of all movement, growth, generation, and noise. It is the "Whir" of value scaling (Quantity).

**System Status:** Existence is the result of the Monad (Limit) clamping down on the Indefinite Dyad (Unlimited). This creates Geometry (Form).
